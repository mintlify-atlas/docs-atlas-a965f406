## General questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does it take to receive my video?">
    Your personalized Harry Potter intro video will be generated and delivered to your email within a few hours after submission. The exact time depends on the resolution you selected and current server load.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What information do I need to provide?">
    To create your video, you'll need to fill in:
    - **Title**: Your name or main text for the intro
    - **Subtitle**: Additional text or tagline
    - **Email address**: Where we'll send your completed video
    - **Resolution**: Choose from SD, HD, or 4K quality
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I create multiple videos?">
    Yes! You can create as many personalized Harry Potter intros as you'd like. Simply submit a new request through the [creator form](https://gostargazer.com/harry-potter/) for each video you want to generate.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Video specifications

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What resolutions are available?">
    The Harry Potter Intro Creator offers three resolution options:
    - **SD (Standard Definition)**: Perfect for quick previews and social media stories
    - **HD (High Definition)**: Great for most YouTube videos and general use
    - **4K (Ultra HD)**: Premium quality for professional projects and maximum detail
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long is the intro video?">
    The intro video follows the iconic Harry Potter opening sequence style, creating a cinematic introduction with your personalized text. The length is optimized to match the classic Harry Potter intro format.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What format will my video be delivered in?">
    Your video will be delivered as a standard video file format (typically MP4) that's compatible with most video players, editing software, and social media platforms.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Usage and rights

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use my video commercially?">
    The videos are created for personal enjoyment and fan content. The Harry Potter brand and style are copyrighted by Warner Bros. Entertainment Inc. Please respect intellectual property rights and use your videos responsibly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit my video after generation?">
    Once your video is delivered, you can download it and use it in your own video editing software. However, you cannot modify the generation request after submission. If you need changes, you'll need to submit a new request.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I made a typo in my submission?">
    Double-check your text before submitting! If you've already submitted and notice an error, you'll need to create a new video request with the correct information. Make sure to verify your email address is correct so you can receive your video.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I haven't received my video. What should I do?">
    If you haven't received your video within a few hours:
    1. Check your spam/junk folder
    2. Verify you entered the correct email address
    3. Wait up to 24 hours for processing during high-demand periods
    4. Contact support if you still haven't received it (see the [support page](/support))
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The video file won't play. How can I fix this?">
    Try these troubleshooting steps:
    - Ensure you have a modern video player installed (VLC Media Player is recommended)
    - Check that the download completed successfully (verify file size)
    - Try opening the file with a different video player
    - Re-download the file if it may have been corrupted
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I request a different resolution after submission?">
    No, you cannot change the resolution after submission. If you need a different resolution, you'll need to submit a new request with your preferred resolution setting.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
