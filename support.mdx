## Common issues

### Video delivery problems

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    First, verify that you're checking the correct email address you provided during submission.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check spam folder">
    Email filters sometimes redirect automated messages to spam or junk folders. Check these folders for your video delivery email.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for processing">
    Videos are typically delivered within a few hours, but during peak times it may take longer. Allow up to 24 hours before reaching out.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If you still haven't received your video after 24 hours, reach out through the Stargazer website for assistance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Video playback issues

<Warning>
  If your video won't play, ensure you have a compatible video player installed. We recommend VLC Media Player, which supports virtually all video formats.
</Warning>

Troubleshooting steps:

1. **Verify the download**: Make sure the file downloaded completely without errors
2. **Check file size**: A very small file size may indicate a corrupt or incomplete download
3. **Try different players**: Test the video in multiple video players (VLC, Windows Media Player, QuickTime)
4. **Re-download**: Delete the file and download it again from the email link
