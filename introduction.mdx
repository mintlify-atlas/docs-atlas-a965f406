The Harry Potter Intro Creator uses a straightforward process:

1. You provide your custom title, subtitle, email, and preferred resolution
2. The system generates your personalized intro video using the iconic Harry Potter visual style
3. Your finished video is delivered to your email inbox within a few hours

No software installation, no video editing experience, and no complex configurations required—just pure magic.
