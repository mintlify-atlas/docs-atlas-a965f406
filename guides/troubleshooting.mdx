## Email delivery issues

### I haven't received my video

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check your spam/junk folder" icon="folder">
    Email filters sometimes categorize delivery emails as spam.

    **How to check:**
    1. Open your email client
    2. Navigate to spam, junk, or bulk mail folder
    3. Search for emails from the delivery domain
    4. Mark as "Not Spam" if found

    <Tip>
      Add the sender to your contacts or safe senders list to prevent future emails from going to spam.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Verify your email address" icon="envelope-circle-check">
    A typo in your email address will prevent delivery.

    **Common mistakes:**
    - Missing characters ("gmil.com" instead of "gmail.com")
    - Transposed letters ("johndoe@gmial.com")
    - Extra spaces before or after the address
    - Wrong domain extension (.con instead of .com)

    <Note>
      If you entered the wrong email address, you'll need to submit a new request with the correct email.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wait for processing to complete" icon="clock">
    Video generation takes time, especially during peak hours.

    **Expected wait times:**
    - **SD resolution:** 1-3 hours
    - **HD resolution:** 2-4 hours
    - **4K resolution:** 3-6 hours

    <Info>
      Processing times may be longer during weekends or holidays when demand is higher.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Check your inbox storage" icon="database">
    A full inbox may reject incoming emails.

    **Solutions:**
    - Delete old emails to free up space
    - Archive important messages
    - Empty your trash/deleted items folder
    - Check your storage quota in account settings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Temporary email addresses" icon="ban">
    Temporary or disposable email services may not work reliably.

    <Warning>
      Avoid using temporary email services (10minutemail, TempMail, etc.) as they may expire before your video is delivered.
    </Warning>

    Use a permanent email address from a reliable provider like Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, or your work/school email.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Still haven't received it?

If you've waited more than 6 hours and checked all the above:

1. Verify you received a submission confirmation
2. Check all folders in your email client (including promotions, updates, social)
3. Search your entire mailbox for keywords like "Harry Potter" or "video"
4. Try submitting a new request with a different email address

## Video quality issues

### The video quality is poor

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your selected resolution">
    The resolution you chose determines the output quality.

    - **SD:** Lower quality, suitable for small screens
    - **HD:** Good quality for most uses
    - **4K:** Maximum quality

    <Tip>
      If you selected SD and need better quality, create a new video with HD or 4K resolution.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your playback device">
    Sometimes the issue is with how you're viewing the video, not the video itself.

    **Troubleshooting playback:**
    - Ensure your screen resolution supports the video quality
    - Use a modern video player (VLC, QuickTime, Windows Media Player)
    - Check your internet connection if streaming
    - Download the video instead of streaming it
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify file integrity">
    The download may have been corrupted.

    **Check if:**
    - File size seems unusually small
    - Video won't play at all
    - There are visual artifacts or glitches

    **Solution:** Re-download the video from the original email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### The video appears pixelated or blurry

<Accordion title="Resolution vs display size mismatch">
  Lower resolution videos will appear pixelated on large displays.

  **Resolution guidelines:**
  - **Mobile phones:** SD is usually sufficient
  - **Computer monitors:** HD recommended
  - **Large displays/projectors:** 4K recommended
  - **4K TVs:** 4K required for best quality
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Compression issues">
  Some platforms compress uploaded videos.

  <Note>
    If uploading to social media, choose the highest quality upload option available. Many platforms have a "HD" or "high quality" toggle when uploading.
  </Note>

  **Platform-specific tips:**
  - **YouTube:** Use 4K source for best results
  - **Instagram:** HD is sufficient; 4K gets compressed
  - **Facebook:** HD recommended
  - **TikTok:** HD or even SD works well
</Accordion>

## Form submission issues

### The form won't submit

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check required fields" icon="list-check">
    All four fields must be completed:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Title">Must not be empty</Step>
      <Step title="Subtitle">Must not be empty</Step>
      <Step title="Email">Must be a valid email format</Step>
      <Step title="Resolution">Must select SD, HD, or 4K</Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email format validation" icon="at">
    Your email must be in a valid format.

    **Valid formats:**
    - user@domain.com
    - firstname.lastname@company.co.uk
    - name123@email-provider.com

    **Invalid formats:**
    - missing @ symbol
    - missing domain
    - spaces in the email
    - special characters like ()[]{};
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Browser issues" icon="browser">
    Try these browser troubleshooting steps:

    1. **Clear cache and cookies**
       - Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data
       - Firefox: Options → Privacy → Clear Data
       - Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

    2. **Disable browser extensions**
       - Ad blockers may interfere with form submission
       - Try disabling extensions temporarily

    3. **Try a different browser**
       - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge are all supported
       - Use incognito/private mode to test

    4. **Update your browser**
       - Ensure you're using the latest version
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Network connectivity" icon="wifi">
    Check your internet connection.

    **Quick tests:**
    - Can you load other websites?
    - Is your Wi-Fi or data connection stable?
    - Try disconnecting and reconnecting
    - Restart your router if necessary
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Error messages

<Warning>
  If you see a specific error message, note the exact text for troubleshooting.
</Warning>

**Common error messages:**

| Error | Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------|----------|
| "Invalid email" | Email format is incorrect | Check for typos and ensure proper format |
| "All fields required" | Missing information | Fill in all four fields |
| "Submission failed" | Network or server issue | Wait a moment and try again |
| "Rate limit exceeded" | Too many requests | Wait 5-10 minutes before trying again |

## Video content issues

### Text doesn't appear correctly

<Accordion title="Special characters">
  Some special characters may not display as expected.

  **Generally safe to use:**
  - Letters (A-Z, a-z)
  - Numbers (0-9)
  - Basic punctuation (. , ! ? - ')
  - Spaces

  **May cause issues:**
  - Emojis
  - Extended Unicode characters
  - Symbols like © ® ™
  - Accented characters (depending on language support)

  <Tip>
    Stick to standard English characters for best compatibility. If you need special characters, test with an SD version first.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Text length">
  Very long titles or subtitles may be truncated or look cramped.

  **Recommended lengths:**
  - **Title:** 1-20 characters
  - **Subtitle:** 1-40 characters

  <Note>
    Shorter text generally looks more impactful and easier to read.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

## Still having problems?

If none of these solutions work:

1. **Document the issue**
   - Note exactly what you're experiencing
   - Take screenshots of any error messages
   - Record the exact steps you took

2. **Verify the service status**
   - Check the [Stargazer website](https://gostargazer.com/harry-potter/) for any announcements
   - Look for maintenance notices or service updates

3. **Try at a different time**
   - The service may be temporarily unavailable
   - Peak times may cause delays
   - Try during off-peak hours (early morning/late evening)

<Info>
  Most issues are resolved by double-checking your email address, waiting the full processing time, and checking your spam folder.
</Info>
