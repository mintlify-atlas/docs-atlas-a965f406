---
title: Creating your video
description: Complete walkthrough of the video creation process from start to finish
---

Follow this step-by-step guide to create your personalized Harry Potter-style intro video.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the creator">
    Visit the [Harry Potter Intro Creator](https://gostargazer.com/harry-potter/) to get started with your magical journey.

    <img src="https://i.imgur.com/MOFvBXn.png" alt="Harry Potter Intro Creator interface" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your title">
    The title is the main text that will appear in your video. This is typically your name or the name of the person or project you're creating the video for.

    <Tip>
      Keep your title concise and impactful. Names work best - for example: "John Watson" or "The Marauders".
    </Tip>

    **Examples of great titles:**
    - Your full name ("Emma Thompson")
    - A character name ("The Chosen One")
    - A team or group name ("Dumbledore's Army")
    - A project title ("The Final Adventure")
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your subtitle">
    The subtitle appears below the main title and provides additional context or flair to your video.

    <Info>
      The subtitle is your chance to add personality and detail. Think of it as your Hogwarts house, title, or magical descriptor.
    </Info>

    **Subtitle ideas:**
    - Your Hogwarts house ("Gryffindor's Bravest")
    - A magical title ("Master of Transfiguration")
    - A descriptive phrase ("Seeker of Ancient Magic")
    - A memorable quote ("Mischief Managed")
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide your email address">
    Enter the email address where you want to receive your finished video. Make sure to use a valid email address that you check regularly.

    <Warning>
      Double-check your email address for typos! The video will be sent to this address within a few hours.
    </Warning>

    Your email address is only used to deliver your video and will not be shared or used for other purposes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your resolution">
    Choose the video quality that best fits your needs:

    <Accordion title="SD (Standard Definition)">
      Best for quick previews, social media stories, or when file size is a concern. Fastest processing time.
      
      **Recommended for:**
      - Quick tests or previews
      - Instagram/TikTok stories
      - Email attachments
      - Limited storage or bandwidth
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="HD (High Definition)">
      The perfect balance between quality and file size. Great for most uses including social media posts and presentations.
      
      **Recommended for:**
      - YouTube videos
      - Facebook/Instagram posts
      - Presentations
      - General sharing
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="4K (Ultra HD)">
      Maximum quality for professional use, large displays, or when you want the absolute best visual experience. Longer processing time and larger file size.
      
      **Recommended for:**
      - Professional projects
      - Large screen displays
      - Future-proofing your content
      - When quality is paramount
    </Accordion>

    <Note>
      Higher resolutions take longer to process and result in larger file sizes. Choose HD for the best balance of quality and convenience.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Review all your information one final time, then click the submit button to generate your video.

    <img src="https://i.imgur.com/aw0pm0T.png" alt="Example of completed Harry Potter intro video" />

    After submission, you'll receive a confirmation that your video is being created.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for email delivery">
    Your personalized Harry Potter intro video will be generated and sent to your email within a few hours.

    <Info>
      Processing time varies based on current demand and your selected resolution. Most videos are delivered within 2-4 hours.
    </Info>

    **What to expect:**
    - You'll receive an email with a download link or the video attached
    - The video will be in MP4 format, ready to use
    - No account creation or login required
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next?

Once you receive your video via email, you can:

- Download it to your device
- Share it on social media platforms
- Use it as an intro for your YouTube videos
- Include it in presentations or projects
- Send it to friends and fellow Potterheads

<Tip>
  Save your video to a safe location after downloading. You can always create new variations with different titles and subtitles!
</Tip>
