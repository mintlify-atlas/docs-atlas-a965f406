## Create your first video

Follow this guide to generate your personalized Harry Potter-style intro video.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Harry Potter Intro Creator">
    Navigate to [gostargazer.com/harry-potter](https://gostargazer.com/harry-potter/) in your web browser.
    
    <img src="https://i.imgur.com/MOFvBXn.png" alt="Harry Potter Intro Creator interface" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in your video details">
    Complete the form with your personalized information:

    **Title**  
    Enter the main text for your intro. This appears as the primary heading in your video.
    
    <Note>
      Consider using your name, project title, or a magical phrase like "The Adventures of [Your Name]".
    </Note>

    **Subtitle**  
    Add supporting text that appears below your title. This can be a tagline, description, or additional magical flourish.

    **Email Address**  
    Provide a valid email where you'll receive your finished video.

    **Resolution**  
    Choose your preferred video quality:
    - **SD** - Standard definition for smaller file sizes
    - **HD** - High definition for most uses (recommended)
    - **4K** - Ultra high definition for maximum quality

    <img src="https://i.imgur.com/aw0pm0T.png" alt="Form fields example" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Click the submit button to send your video request. You'll receive a confirmation that your video is being generated.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive your video">
    Your personalized Harry Potter intro video will be delivered to your email within a few hours.
    
    <Info>
      Processing time depends on current queue and video resolution. 4K videos may take slightly longer than SD or HD.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's next?

Once you receive your video, you can:

- Use it as an intro for YouTube videos or social media content
- Add it to film projects or presentations
- Share it with fellow Harry Potter fans
- Create multiple variations with different titles and subtitles

<Warning>
  Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't receive your video within a few hours. Add the sender to your contacts to ensure delivery.
</Warning>
