Create a truly magical intro by customizing the text that appears in your Harry Potter-style video. The Harry Potter Intro Creator lets you personalize both the title and subtitle to tell your own wizarding story.

## Text fields

Your intro video includes two customizable text fields that appear during the animation:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Title" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    The main text that appears prominently in your intro. This is typically your name, house, or main message.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Subtitle" icon="text">
    Secondary text that provides additional context or a tagline. This appears below the title in a smaller font.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Best practices for text

Follow these guidelines to create the most impactful intro:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep it concise">
    Shorter text creates more dramatic impact. The Harry Potter aesthetic works best with brief, punchy phrases that match the cinematic style of the films.
    
    <Tip>
      For titles, aim for 1-4 words. For subtitles, keep it under 8-10 words for optimal readability.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Consider the pacing">
    Your text appears with magical animation effects. Longer text may feel rushed or cramped on screen.
    
    <Info>
      Test how your text sounds when spoken aloud slowly and dramatically - that's roughly how it will feel in the video.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use proper capitalization">
    The generator preserves your exact capitalization choices. You can use:
    - ALL CAPS for dramatic emphasis
    - Title Case for a classic look
    - lowercase for a modern feel
    
    <Note>
      The original Harry Potter intros use Title Case, so this tends to look most authentic.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Avoid special characters">
    Stick to standard letters, numbers, and basic punctuation (periods, commas, apostrophes). Emojis and complex symbols may not render correctly in the final video.
    
    <Warning>
      Unusual Unicode characters or special symbols might cause rendering issues or be replaced with standard characters.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Character limits

While there are no hard technical limits enforced by the form, the visual design of the intro works best within these recommendations:

| Field | Recommended Maximum | What Happens If Exceeded |
|-------|-------------------|-------------------------|
| Title | 30 characters | Text may appear smaller or wrap to multiple lines |
| Subtitle | 50 characters | May overlap with other visual elements |

<Warning>
  Extremely long text (over 100 characters) may be truncated or cause the rendering process to fail. Always preview your choices mentally before submitting.
</Warning>

## Preview your choices

Since there's no live preview available, here's how to mentally test your customization:

1. **Read it aloud** in your best dramatic voice
2. **Imagine it** on a dark, starry background with magical particle effects
3. **Consider the timing** - your text appears over approximately 8-10 seconds
4. **Check for typos** - you'll receive the final video via email, so make sure everything is spelled correctly before submitting

<Tip>
  Write your text in a notes app first, then copy it into the form. This gives you time to refine your wording and catch any errors.
</Tip>

## Editing after submission

Once you submit your customization choices:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submission is final">
    The video generation process begins immediately. You cannot edit the text after clicking submit.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create a new video">
    If you notice a mistake or want to change your text, you'll need to create a new video by submitting another request through the form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="No additional cost">
    The service is free, so you can generate multiple versions with different text to find the perfect combination.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Each submission generates a unique video, so you can create multiple versions with different customizations for different purposes.
</Note>
