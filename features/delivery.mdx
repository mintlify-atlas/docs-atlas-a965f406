After you customize and submit your Harry Potter intro, the video is generated on the server and delivered directly to your email address. Understanding the delivery process helps you know what to expect and when to anticipate your magical creation.

## How delivery works

The video generation and delivery process follows these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Form submission">
    You fill out the form with your title, subtitle, email address, and resolution choice, then click submit.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Queue processing">
    Your video request enters a processing queue. Videos are generated in the order they're received.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Video rendering">
    The system creates your personalized intro with your custom text, applying all the magical effects and animations.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Email delivery">
    Once rendering is complete, the video is sent directly to the email address you provided.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  The entire process typically takes "a few hours" according to the service description. This timeframe varies based on resolution choice and current server load.
</Info>

## Expected wait times

Here's what you can expect for delivery timing:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="By resolution">
    Different resolutions have different processing times:
    
    | Resolution | Typical Delivery Time |
    |-----------|----------------------|
    | SD (480p) | 1-2 hours |
    | HD (1080p) | 2-4 hours |
    | 4K (2160p) | 4-8 hours |
    
    <Note>
      These are average times under normal conditions. During peak usage, times may be longer.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Time of day factors">
    When you submit can affect processing time:
    
    **Faster processing (off-peak):**
    - Late evening (10 PM - 2 AM)
    - Early morning (5 AM - 8 AM)
    - Weekday mornings
    
    **Slower processing (peak):**
    - Afternoon (2 PM - 6 PM)
    - Weekend afternoons
    - Holiday periods
    
    <Tip>
      If you need your video by a specific time, submit it at least 12 hours in advance to account for potential delays.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Maximum wait time">
    While most videos arrive within the expected timeframe:
    
    - **Normal delivery:** Within 2-8 hours depending on resolution
    - **Extended delivery:** Up to 24 hours during high demand
    - **If over 24 hours:** Check your spam folder or contact support
    
    <Warning>
      If you haven't received your video within 24 hours, there may be an issue. Check the troubleshooting section below.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## What the email contains

When your video is ready, you'll receive an email with:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Video file attachment or link">
    The email includes your personalized Harry Potter intro video, either:
    
    - **Attached directly** (for SD and some HD videos)
    - **As a download link** (for larger HD and all 4K videos)
    
    The method depends on file size. Larger files are typically provided via a download link to ensure reliable delivery.
    
    <Info>
      Download links are typically valid for 30-90 days. Download your video promptly and save it to your device or cloud storage.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Video specifications">
    The email should confirm:
    
    - Your chosen resolution (SD, HD, or 4K)
    - Video duration (typically 8-12 seconds)
    - File format (usually MP4 for maximum compatibility)
    - File size
    
    These details help you verify you received the correct resolution.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Usage information">
    The email may include:
    
    - How to use your video
    - Sharing suggestions
    - Social media recommendations
    - Link back to the Harry Potter Intro Creator
    
    <Tip>
      Save this email in a dedicated folder so you can easily find the video and download link later if needed.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Checking your email

After submitting your request, monitor your email for delivery:

### Where to look

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Primary inbox" icon="inbox">

  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Spam/junk folder" icon="folder">
    Automated emails sometimes get filtered. If you don't see the email in your inbox after several hours, check spam.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Promotions tab" icon="tags">
    Gmail users should check the Promotions tab, where automated service emails often land.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Updates folder" icon="envelope">
    Some email clients categorize service notifications in an Updates or Notifications folder.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  Always check your spam/junk folder before assuming your video wasn't delivered. Mark the email as "Not Spam" to ensure future videos arrive in your inbox.
</Warning>

## Email address accuracy

Ensuring you enter the correct email address is critical:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Common mistakes">
    Double-check for these frequent errors:
    
    - Typos in your email address (gmail.com vs gamil.com)
    - Missing characters (@, dots, etc.)
    - Wrong domain (.com vs .net vs .org)
    - Autocorrect changing your email
    - Extra spaces before or after the address
    
    <Tip>
      Copy and paste your email address from another source instead of typing it manually to avoid typos.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Best practices">
    Follow these tips for successful delivery:
    
    1. **Use a reliable email provider** (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
    2. **Verify before submitting** - read your email address carefully
    3. **Use a personal email** you check regularly
    4. **Avoid temporary email services** that may expire or block attachments
    5. **Check spam settings** aren't too aggressive
    
    <Note>
      There's no email confirmation after you submit the form. Your first notification will be when the video is ready and delivered.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="If you made a mistake">
    If you realize you entered the wrong email address:
    
    ❌ **Cannot be corrected:** Once submitted, you cannot change the email address
    
    ✅ **Solution:** Submit a new request with:
    - The correct email address
    - Same title and subtitle
    - Same or different resolution
    
    <Warning>
      If you entered an incorrect email address, the video will be delivered to that address (if it exists) or will fail to deliver. You cannot retrieve a video sent to the wrong email.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Downloading your video

Once you receive the email:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the email">
    Click on the email from the Harry Potter Intro Creator service.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download the video">
    Either:
    - Click the download link (for large files)
    - Download the attachment (for smaller files)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save to safe location">
    Store the video file in a permanent location:
    - Your computer's Videos folder
    - Cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox, iCloud)
    - External hard drive for backup
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify the video">
    Open and play the video to ensure:
    - Your customization is correct
    - The resolution is what you selected
    - The video plays properly
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Download and save your video immediately upon receipt. Download links may expire, and you don't want to lose access to your magical creation.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting delivery issues

If you don't receive your video:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Video hasn't arrived yet">
    If you're still waiting for your video:
    
    1. **Check the time** - Confirm it's been at least:
       - 2 hours for SD
       - 4 hours for HD
       - 8 hours for 4K
    
    2. **Check all email folders** - Inbox, Spam, Promotions, Updates
    
    3. **Wait 24 hours** - Processing can take up to a day during peak times
    
    4. **Verify email address** - Confirm you entered it correctly (check for typos)
    
    <Info>
      Most delivery issues resolve within 24 hours. Be patient, especially if you submitted during a weekend or holiday.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Email address was incorrect">
    If you realize you made a typo:
    
    **Problem:** You cannot retrieve a video sent to the wrong address
    
    **Solution:**
    1. Submit a new request with the correct email
    2. Use the same customization (title/subtitle)
    3. Wait for the new video to be generated
    
    <Note>
      The service is free, so there's no cost to submit a corrected request.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Download link expired">
    If the download link in your email no longer works:
    
    **Problem:** Links typically expire after 30-90 days
    
    **Solution:**
    - Submit a new request with the same customization
    - Always save videos immediately after receipt to avoid this issue
    
    <Warning>
      There's no way to retrieve an expired download link. You must generate a new video.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Video file is corrupted">
    If the video won't play or appears corrupted:
    
    1. **Try different video players** (VLC, QuickTime, Windows Media Player)
    2. **Re-download** the file - the download may have been interrupted
    3. **Check file size** - Compare to the expected size for your resolution
    4. **Request a new video** if the problem persists
    
    <Tip>
      VLC Media Player can often play videos that won't open in default players. Download it for free if you encounter playback issues.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Multiple videos

You can create and receive multiple videos:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Same email address" icon="envelope">
    Submit multiple requests using the same email address. Each video will be delivered separately as it completes.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Different customizations" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Create different versions with different text to test ideas or serve different purposes.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Multiple resolutions" icon="film">
    Generate the same video in SD, HD, and 4K by submitting three separate requests with identical text.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="No limits" icon="infinity">
    The service is free with no apparent limits on how many videos you can create.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Each request is processed independently. Submitting multiple requests doesn't speed up or slow down delivery of any individual video.
</Note>

## After delivery

Once you've successfully received your video:

1. **Download immediately** - Don't rely solely on the email
2. **Backup your video** - Save copies to multiple locations
3. **Share your creation** - Use it for its intended purpose
4. **Create more videos** - Try different customizations for different needs

<Tip>
  Consider organizing your Harry Potter intro videos in a dedicated folder with descriptive filenames like "HP-Intro-Birthday-HD.mp4" so you can easily find specific versions later.
</Tip>
