## Resolution comparison

Here's a detailed breakdown of each resolution option:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="SD (480p)">
    ### Standard Definition
    
    **Resolution:** 854 x 480 pixels
    
    **Best for:**
    - Social media stories (Instagram, Snapchat)
    - Quick previews and drafts
    - Mobile viewing
    - Situations where file size matters
    
    **Advantages:**
    - ⚡ Fastest processing time (typically 1-2 hours)
    - 📦 Smallest file size (usually 5-15 MB)
    - 📱 Easy to share via messaging apps
    - 💾 Minimal storage requirements
    
    **Limitations:**
    - May appear pixelated on large screens
    - Not suitable for professional productions
    - Less detail in magical effects
    
    <Tip>
      Choose SD if you need your video quickly or plan to share it on mobile platforms where high resolution isn't critical.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="HD (1080p)">
    ### High Definition
    
    **Resolution:** 1920 x 1080 pixels
    
    **Best for:**
    - YouTube videos
    - Social media posts (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn)
    - Website embeds
    - General sharing and viewing
    
    **Advantages:**
    - 🎬 Excellent quality for most uses
    - ⚖️ Good balance of quality and file size
    - 💻 Sharp on most computer monitors
    - 📺 Perfect for HD TVs and projectors
    
    **File characteristics:**
    - Processing time: 2-4 hours typically
    - File size: 20-50 MB
    - Compatible with all modern devices and platforms
    
    <Info>
      HD is the most popular choice and recommended for most users. It provides professional-quality results without excessive wait times or file sizes.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="4K (2160p)">
    ### Ultra HD (4K)
    
    **Resolution:** 3840 x 2160 pixels
    
    **Best for:**
    - Professional film projects
    - Cinema screenings
    - 4K displays and TVs
    - Future-proofing your content
    - Maximum quality preservation
    
    **Advantages:**
    - 🌟 Highest possible quality
    - 🔍 Incredible detail in every frame
    - ✨ Magical effects appear most vivid
    - 🎥 Professional-grade output
    
    **Considerations:**
    - ⏱️ Longest processing time (4-8 hours)
    - 📦 Large file size (100-300 MB)
    - 💾 Requires significant storage space
    - 🌐 May be difficult to share via email or messaging
    
    <Warning>
      4K videos take significantly longer to process and result in much larger files. Only choose this option if you truly need ultra-high definition quality.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Choosing the right resolution

Use this decision tree to select the best resolution for your needs:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Consider your final output">
    Where will your video be displayed?
    
    - **Mobile only** → SD is sufficient
    - **Web and social media** → HD is ideal
    - **Cinema or 4K TV** → Choose 4K
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Think about timing">
    How quickly do you need the video?
    
    - **Within a few hours** → SD
    - **Same day** → HD
    - **Can wait** → 4K is fine
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check your storage">
    How much space do you have available?
    
    - **Limited storage** → SD
    - **Moderate storage** → HD
    - **Plenty of storage** → 4K
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Assess quality needs">
    What's your quality requirement?
    
    - **Good enough** → SD
    - **Professional** → HD
    - **Maximum quality** → 4K
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  When in doubt, choose HD (1080p). It provides the best balance of quality, processing time, and file size for most use cases.
</Note>

## Processing times

Processing times vary based on resolution and current server load:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Typical processing times">
    These are average processing times under normal conditions:
    
    | Resolution | Average Time | Range |
    |-----------|--------------|-------|
    | SD (480p) | 1.5 hours | 1-2 hours |
    | HD (1080p) | 3 hours | 2-4 hours |
    | 4K (2160p) | 6 hours | 4-8 hours |
    
    <Info>
      All videos are typically delivered within "a few hours" as stated in the service description. Plan accordingly based on your needs.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Factors affecting processing time">
    Several factors can influence how long your video takes to generate:
    
    - **Queue position:** Videos are processed in order of submission
    - **Server load:** High traffic periods may slow processing
    - **Resolution choice:** Higher resolutions require more computation
    - **Complexity:** Certain text combinations may require additional processing
    
    <Tip>
      Submit your video during off-peak hours (early morning or late evening) for potentially faster processing.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="If processing takes longer than expected">
    Most videos arrive within the expected timeframe, but if yours takes longer:
    
    1. Check your email spam/junk folder
    2. Wait up to 24 hours before being concerned
    3. Ensure you entered your email address correctly
    4. Higher resolutions during peak times may take longer
    
    <Warning>
      Processing times can extend up to 24 hours during periods of very high demand. The service aims for "a few hours" but this is not guaranteed.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

  You can always generate the same video in multiple resolutions. Try SD first to preview your customization, then create an HD or 4K version if needed.
</Tip>

## Cannot change after submission

Once you submit your video request:

<Warning>
  The resolution is locked in and cannot be changed. If you want the same video in a different resolution, you must submit a new request with the same text customization.
</Warning>

To create multiple resolution versions:

1. Submit your first request with your chosen resolution
2. Wait for delivery confirmation
3. Submit another request with identical text but different resolution
4. You'll receive separate emails with each version
